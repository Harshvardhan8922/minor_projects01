Flipkart: India's Ultimate One-Stop Online Shopping Destination

Welcome to Flipkart, India's trusted and beloved e-commerce platform, revolutionising online shopping since 2007. With over 200 million users, 150 million+ products across 80+ categories, Flipkart isn't just an online store; it's your digital shopping companion.

What Can You Buy from Flipkart?

Flipkart's strength lies in its incredible diversity. With offerings for every need, aspiration, and budget, you can dive into meticulously curated categories for your needs:

Mobile and Electronics

Flipkart offers a wide range of smartphones from brands like Samsung, Apple, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Vivo, and Oppo. Explore budget-friendly smartphones, gaming phones, refurbished options, and accessories such as back covers, earbuds, smartwatches, and fitness bands.

You can also shop laptops, gaming laptops, tablets, bluetooth speakers, headphones, digital cameras, printers, and smart home devices.

Home and Kitchen Appliances

Upgrade your home with Smart TVs, home theatre systems, soundbars, and essential appliances like refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, water purifiers, and geysers.

Equip your kitchen with microwaves, mixer-grinders, air fryers, electric kettles, coffee makers, and more. Maintain cleanliness with vacuum cleaners, irons, heaters, fans, and air purifiers.

Fashion and Beauty

Flipkart is your fashion destination for clothing, footwear, and accessories for men, women, and kids. Shop ethnic wear, western wear, activewear, and accessorize with handbags, watches, sunglasses, and jewellery.

Choose from a wide range of beauty products including skincare, makeup, haircare, fragrances, and grooming essentials.

Home and Furniture

Enhance your living space with home decor, home lighting, curtains, rugs, wall paintings, photo frames, and mirrors.

Discover comfortable sofa sets, dining tables, cupboards, modular kitchens, and premium mattresses designed for durability and comfort.

Daily Essentials

Stock up on household supplies, groceries, baby products, diapers, toys, books, school supplies, and maternity care products.

Sports Equipment and Two Wheelers

Explore scooters, motorcycles, and electric vehicles (EVs), along with sports equipment for cricket, football, badminton, cycling, fitness gear, video games, and musical instruments.

Gift Cards

Flipkart Gift Cards and e-Gift vouchers offer flexibility for birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, festivals, and corporate gifting. Available instantly via email or SMS.

Flipkart Reset and Exchange Program: Trade-in Your Old Device

Flipkart Reset offers you the opportunity to sell your old phone at the best price. You can also get an extra 4% value on your phone if you choose Flipkart Gift Card at checkout. Not only is this an effective way to sell old phones, but it also helps reduce electronic waste.

Provide your phone details, set up an appointment with an executive who will evaluate your phone at pickup and offer a final price. Money will be paid instantly.

Check out Flipkart Exchange Store to exchange your old electronic device for a new one on Flipkart. You can exchange laptops, phones, TVs, washing machines, mixers, refrigerators, tablets, and ACs. Even non-working products are accepted.
